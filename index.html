Research Project: Evaluation of Pollster Accuracy

Link to Research Project Github

Evaluation of Pollster Accuracy is a research project I helped make in COGS 108. Our project looks into which polling sources are the most accurate at predicting the outcomes of U.S. federal and gubernatorial elections.


Music Project: Machine Learning for Music Research

The Machine Learning for Music Research is a research project on machine learning models for music generation. For this project I researched transformer models based on the Pop Music Transformer, updated the code to train the model on the Pop1K7 dataset, and generated my own music.
